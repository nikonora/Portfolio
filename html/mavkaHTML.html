Mavka Diana

The history of Diana began in the 17th century, in a small Ukrainian village in the Carpathians. She was born in an ordinary family, was an ordinary happy girl. And she had a best trend Ostap, with whom she spent all her free time. For him, this friendship grew into something more, but soon the boy moved to the city. A couple of years after the friend moved, Diana met a young man and fell in love... The day before their wedding, Ostap arrived, who had been in love with Diana all this time. The girl agreed to meet him in the evening near their favourite lake in the forest.

And no one will ever know that it was on this evening, the day before the wedding, that her best childhood friend would drown her because she did not give her heart to him.

Diana, after her death, turned into a mavka, a forest mermaid, who to this day lures all the guys into the water in search of her beloved, whose wife she will never become.
